Ukraine is ahead of France in the number of new patients with COVID-19

TUESDAY, 17 NOVEMBER 2020, 05:24

Stepanov:

If the quarantine is lifted, we will have up to 30,000 patients a day

According to the number of new cases of coronavirus, Ukraine ranks 12th among other countries, France - 13th.

Source: Worldometers data

Details: On Sunday, the coronavirus was detected in 9,832 Ukrainians. In France, confirmed almost 400 cases less. However, the number of victims of the disease during this time differs significantly: 94 deaths in Ukraine and 506 in France.

At the same time , a sharp outbreak of COVID-19 has recently been observed in France .

During the pandemic, almost 546,000 Ukrainians were diagnosed with coronavirus. In France, 1.9 million people have already become infected.

In total, 504.5 thousand cases of infection and 7305 deaths have been confirmed in the world over the past 24 hours.

Health Minister Maksym Stepanov stressed that if the weekend quarantine and other anti-epidemic measures are lifted, there will be up to 30,000 coronavirus patients a day in Ukraine every day.

Source : Stepanov on the air "Freedom of Speech"

Stepanov's direct speech : If we cancel anti-epidemic measures (including weekend quarantine), we will see an increase to 20-25-30 thousand patients (per day - ed.). Then we will very quickly get 100% occupancy of beds in our hospitals and the collapse of the entire medical system.

No country in the world has come up with another way to curb the epidemic than the introduction of anti-epidemic measuresâ€¦

Our European neighbors are taking strict quarantine measures. "

Details : He noted that the best way is complete quarantine, but the state is not able to go for it, according to the calculations of the Ministry of Finance and the Ministry of Economy.

Prehistory

  • On November 11, the Cabinet of Ministers lifted adaptive quarantine, tightened routine quarantine throughout Ukraine, and imposed a full "lockdown" on the weekend of November 14-30 , at least.
  • According to Ukrayinska Pravda, the government was immediately ready to extend the weekend quarantine until December 31 .
